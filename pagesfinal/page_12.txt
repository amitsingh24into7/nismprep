0% Which of the following is closest to the forward price of a share, if Cash Price =

Rs.750, Forward Contract Maturity = 6 months from date, Market Interest rate = 12% pa.
795
840
940.8
772.5

% WRONG ANSWER
CORRECT ANSWER:

795

Explanation:

Forward/futures price of share can be calulated by adding the interest cost to its current price.
6 months maturity means half year of interest cost.

12% of 750 = Rs. 90 is the full year interest cost

Half year interest cost = 90/2 = 45

750 +45 = 795 is closest to six month forward price
